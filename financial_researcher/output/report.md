**Company Overview**

Apple Inc. was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. The company is headquartered in Cupertino, California, and has over 137,000 employees worldwide. Apple designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.

**Financial Performance**

As of December 2022, Apple's revenue was $394 billion, with gross margin of 26.4%. The company generated $94 billion in operating income and $24.3 billion in net income. Apple has consistently delivered strong financial results, driven by the success of its product lines, including the iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and AirPods.

Apple's revenue breakdown is as follows:

* **iPhone**: 52% (2022)
* **Services**: 16% (2022)
* **Mac**: 8% (2022)
* **iPad**: 5% (2022)
* ** wearables, Home and Accessories**: 10% (2022)
